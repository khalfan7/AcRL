Step 2

Thermal simulator

  • Nonlinear RC dynamics with solar gain, wind, and stack effect.
  • Domain randomisation of C, U per episode; stratified W/Sp/Su/F sampling.
  • envs/simulator.py
Tink+1 = Tink + (Δt/C)[Ueff(Tout−Tin) + αGHI − Qhvac]
Step 3

HVAC RL environment

  • 14-dim obs, action ∈ [−1, 1] → ±3 kW HVAC.
  • Comfort = |Tin−Tset|; penalises deviation from 22 °C.
  • COP-adjusted energy cost scaled by monthly price.
  • envs/environment.py
R = −(wc|Tin−Tset| + we|a|·p/pmax + ws|a−aprev|)
